  \ :code:`modularFactor(f, d)`\  chooses a "good" prime and returns the factorization of \ :code:`f`\  modulo this prime in a form that may be used by \ :ref:`completeHensel <l47656e6572616c48656e73656c5061636b616765-636f6d706c65746548656e73656c>`\ .
