OrderlyDifferentialPolynomial R
===============================

:viewcode:`dpolcat.spad line 450 <dpolcat.spad#L450>` :editcode:`[edit on github] <dpolcat.spad#L450>`

* R: \ :ref:`Ring <l52696e67>`\

The ranking on the differential indeterminate is orderly. This is analogous to the domain \ :ref:`Polynomial <l506f6c796e6f6d69616c>`\ .
